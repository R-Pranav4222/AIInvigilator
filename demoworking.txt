How to Demo (Super Simple)
The Setup
Your laptop = The server (runs the project + ML processing)
Your friends' devices (laptop/phone/anything with a browser) = They just open a URL in their browser
Step-by-Step
Step 1: Find your laptop's WiFi IP address

Open a terminal and run:

ipconfig
Look for your WiFi adapter's IPv4 Address — it'll be something like 192.168.1.105 or 192.168.0.15.

Step 2: Make sure the server accepts external connections

Right now python manage.py runserver only listens on 127.0.0.1 (localhost = only your laptop). You need to run it as:

python manage.py runserver 0.0.0.0:8000
The 0.0.0.0 means "accept connections from any device on the network."

Step 3: Connect all devices to the SAME WiFi

You, your two friends, and your teacher's device must all be on the same WiFi network.

Step 4: Your friends open a browser and go to:

http://192.168.1.105:8000/
(Replace with YOUR actual IP from Step 1)

That's it! They see the full app — no downloads, no installs.
